FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hotel booking, resort stays, payments, cancellations, and guest support.

What does Stackly Resorts help with?+
Stackly helps guests discover verified hotels and resorts, compare amenities, reserve rooms, add experiences, and manage booking details from one dashboard.
Are hotels and resorts verified?+
Featured stays are checked for location, room quality, amenities, policies, availability, guest comfort, and pricing clarity before promotion.
Can I add transfers or activities?+
Yes. You can add airport pickup, dining plans, spa slots, guided tours, celebration setups, and other resort experiences when available.
Do you help with booking documents?+
Yes. Your dashboard keeps booking vouchers, payment updates, guest ID reminders, cancellation terms, and check-in instructions organized.
Can hotels manage bookings?+
Partner hotels can manage rooms, booking requests, guest notes, schedules, and follow-ups from the dashboard.
