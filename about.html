Our Journey

Stackly Milestones

A timeline of innovation, growth, and customer achievements since our foundation in 2009.

2009

Local Foundation

Started as a small local technical tutoring room in Salem, TN with 5 agents and 20 customers.

2014

Digital Expansion

Launched our online hotel booking catalog, making verified stay options available to 20K+ guests.

2019

Collaborative Workspaces

Deployed real-time code-editor sandboxes and whiteboards to support peer-to-peer live showrooms.

2024

AI Diagnosis Engine

Integrated our intelligent customer progress advisor, helping over 220,000 global scholars reach their goals.
